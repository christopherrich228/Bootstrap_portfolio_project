Lisbon is the capital and largest city in Portugal. This historic city offers everything from museums, gardens, and stunning architecture to mordern shops and boutiques and a lively nightlife scene! There are mulitple restaurants and fresh food markets where you can try some of the best local cusine and patries! Too much to see by foot? Hop aboard the Tram 28 and take a tour through some of the most popular and historic neighborhoods in the city! If you're feeling tired at the end of a long day of exploring, try a sunset boat tour on the river. There's always simething amazing to see and do in Lisbon!

Events & Attractions

  • Hop aboard the Tram 28 and take a tour of some of the city's most notable neighborhoods like Baixa and Bairro Alto. You can also ride the tram to the hiily streets of the historic Alfama neighborhood. Just make sure to arrive early, as the tram is very popular and cars fill up fast!
  • Speaking of Alfama, perched atop the highest hill in the neighborhood is Castelo de São Jorge. The castle served as a fortification for the Romans, Visigoths and the Moors, who turned it into a royal palace before it was eventually taken by Portugal's first king, Afonso Henriques. The castle also includes a restaurant and archaeological museum.
  • Too many options to choose from and can't settle on just one? That's ok! Check out one Lisbon's tours to see multiple sites, and even explore some locations where tourists rarely venture!

Dining

  • If you're someone who likes a variety of types of food to choose from, look no further than Time Out Market ! No trip to Lisbon is complete without a visit to this iconic food market. With dozens of bars and restaurants, you're bound to find something here to satisfy your taste buds.
  • For those who prefer breathtaking views with a delicious dinner, Chapitô à Mesa is the perfect choice! Located just below São Jorge Castle, Chapitô à Mesa offers gorgeous views of the city and river, while offering a menu with local, vegan, and Mediterranean options. Don't want to view the scenery through a window? No worries! There is also the option to sit outside on the terrace or belvedere area!
  • If expensive, fine dining is more your taste, Belcanto is the perfect choice! With two Michelin Stars, as well landing a spot on The World’s 50 Best Restaurants List, Belcanto remains one of the finest restaurants in Lisbon. Just make sure to book in advance! Tables go fast!

Hotels

  • If luxury is what you're looking for on a vacation, then look no further than Olissippo Lapa Palace. This gorgeous 19th century palace is built on a hilltop with beautiful views of the Tagus River. Whether you prefer to lounge at the pool, relax in the sauna, or explore the gorgeous grounds, Olissippo Lapa Palace offers a one of a kind stay!
  • For a more upbeat, party atmosphere, EVOLUTION Lisboa Hotel is a must stay! Located in the heart of Saldanha’s shopping and business district, this hotel offers live music and entertainment for it's guests!
  • If you're looking for a more affordable and modern hotel, Brown's Downtown Hotel might be the choice for you! Conveniently located in the middle of all the hustle and bustle, this hotel offers a relaxing environment, and each room is up to date with the most modern technology.
