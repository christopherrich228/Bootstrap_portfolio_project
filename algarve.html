The Algarve is Portugal's southernmost region. Boasting some of the most beautiful beaches in the country, it is one of the most popular vacation destinations in all of Europe. Take a trip to Farro, the capital of the region, and see the historic of cathedrals and cobblestone streets of Old Town. If you want gorgeous scenery, Farol da Ponta da Piedade, located just outside Lagos, offers the most incredible panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean as well as the coastal rock formations. With so many breathtakingly gorgeous beaches, it's really hard to go wrong with whichever one you choose!
